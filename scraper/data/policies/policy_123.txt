Policy 123: Policy on Standard Business Hours


This policy addresses the standard business hours for the university.

N/A

Standard business hours for the university are 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday. In addition, some departments may require extended or alternate hours to address the unique needs of their service area.

Individual employee hours to be worked are set by department heads responsible for each area and are not the subject of this policy.
